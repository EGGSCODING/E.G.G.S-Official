What E.G.G.S is
E.G.G.S stands for Epic Gamer Game Squad — a small creative collective that codes and creates gaming videos. We build mods, record gameplay, and share tips and edits for the community.

What does E.G.G.S do?

We code and create gaming videos. Our work includes modding, tutorial videos, highlight reels, and live streams. We focus on collaboration: designers, coders, and creators working together to make things that gamers love.
